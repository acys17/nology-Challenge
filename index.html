Who Am I?

I am a BSc (Hons) Zoology graduate from the University of Manchester. Having loved maths and logical thinking from an early age, here I developed my research and analytical skills culminating in a metanalysis of levels of aggression in primates using R software to perform my analyses.
I previously edited and published research papers for the online scientific journal ScientificReports at SpringerNature Publishing House Ltd. I was responsible for ensuring that publication targets were met in a fast-paced yet high-quality manner. Here I realised I have an affinity for technology and picking up new skills and learning how to use new software.
I am an ex-Police Officer for the Metropolitan Police Service, working in front-line policing in Westminster. Here I developed my interpersonal skills and became well practised in handling high-pressure situations through conducting investigations and interviews, managing crime scenes policing protests and enforcing lockdown restrictions to name a few
